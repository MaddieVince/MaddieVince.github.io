Who am I?

Engineer by day, budding Web Developer by night.

Maddie has spent the formative years of her career designing industrial structures for several Resources & Manufacturing clients across the east coast of Australia. In an effort to combine her current engineering design skillset with the agile and dynamic capability that Technology offers in automation and streamlining work processes, Maddie has now embarked on an intensive training program to upskill in several programming languages.

This portfolio of work is a collection of projects that will be developed over the next 6 months while participating in the SheCodes Plus Program.

Outside of design and learning to code, Maddie enjoys playing video games, reading, learning how to bullet journal and baking.
